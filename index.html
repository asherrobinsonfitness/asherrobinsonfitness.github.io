Common Questions

You have no client results. How do I know this works?

You’re right, I don’t. What I have is my own transformation: 171 lbs at ~17% body fat down to 155 lbs at ~9% in about 8 weeks, coaching myself the exact same way I’ll coach you. If you don’t see measurable fat loss in the first 30 days, you’ll get a full refund. This way, you’re not taking the risk — I am.

I’ve tried this before and it didn’t work.

Whatever you’ve tried before was likely built for a perfect week. My coaching is built for a real one. You weigh yourself, log your food, track your workouts, and show up to your weekly check-in — I adjust everything else around your life. If your last attempt had worked with your schedule, you’d still be doing it.

Why is it only $50?

$50 is the first month, then $199/month after. The first month is discounted because I want little financial risk on your end while you find out if this works for you.

Do you handle the diet side too?

Yes. You get a daily calorie target and a daily protein target, plus a custom meal plan with a flexible structure and swappable alternatives from a 5,000+ recipe library. Nothing is off-limits — I'll suggest specific options when it makes sense, but you're never locked into foods you don't want.

How much time does this take each week?

3–4 gym sessions per week, 45 to 60 minutes each. A couple of minutes a day to weigh yourself and log your food. A once-weekly check-in with me that takes less than 30 minutes. That’s it.

What happens after I pay?

After you pay, you'll be redirected to book your onboarding call and then download the app. Your workouts, nutrition logging, check-ins, and messaging with me are all in one place.

How does the refund actually work?

You can cancel anytime from the app, or message me to do it for you. If you’re inside the first 30 days, you’ll receive a full refund. No form, no waiting period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contract, no cancellation fee.
